Model Assumptions & Accuracy

In the spirit of scientific honesty, here is exactly what this tool computes rigorously, what it estimates with simplified models, and what is illustrative or speculative — nothing here claims to be more certain than it is.

rigorousSurface gravity & escape velocityExact Newtonian formulas (g = M/R², v = √(2GM/R)).
rigorousEquilibrium temperatureStandard formula T = 278·L¼·d−½·(1−A)¼. The formula is exact, but stellar luminosity is a class-typical value and albedo is fixed at 0.30.
groundedAtmosphere retention (cosmic shoreline)Escape velocity vs stellar radiation, scaling as v⁴ — consistent with the Zahnle & Catling (2017) relation; the threshold constant is tuned.
estimateGreenhouse warmingA simplified heuristic from gas fractions — not full radiative transfer. Atmospheric pressure is not modelled (composition is mole-fractions only).
estimateRadiation level & habitability indexProxy models with hand-tuned, Earth-referenced weightings. The habitability index is biased toward Earth-like, water-and-carbon life.
illustrativePlanet visualsSurfaces, clouds, rings and the interior cutaway are physically-motivated artwork, not measured imagery. Atmosphere colour does follow real optics (blue Rayleigh scattering; methane absorbs red → teal).
speculativeBiosphere & field reportInformed astrobiology hypotheses, always possibility-framed — never a claim that life exists.
real dataNearest known analogNASA Exoplanet Archive, matched on measured size, mass, orbit & star type — not atmosphere (which is unmeasured for most real worlds).
