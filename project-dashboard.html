Day 1 Sunday, 8 June 2026

💡 The project is born

🚀 Defined the concept

Settled on the idea: an Exoplanet Biosphere Generator — design a planet, let physics predict its conditions, then let AI imagine the life that could evolve there. Goal: a 10-day astrophysics project that shows real interest and hard work.

🔍 Reviewed the idea & got feedback

Confirmed it's a strong concept. Key feedback: separate the rigorous physics from the speculative biology, and stay scientifically honest. Refined the methane / red-dwarf example (photosynthesis isn't impossible — it would just use red/black pigments).

🎯 Set scope & success criteria

Agreed the project should be a polished, shareable portfolio website — visually impressive and scientifically defensible.
